Welcome to my page! 

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at the Institute for International Economic Studies (IIES), Stockholm University. In Spring 2025, I was a Visiting Research Fellow in the Economics Department at Harvard University. 

My research focuses on macroeconomics, with a particular emphasis on how micro-heterogeneity shapes aggregate outcomes. 

In current work, I combine theoretical modelling, micro-data, and quantitative methods to study how firm-level shocks and market structure shape productivity growth.


**I am on the 2025/26 Academic Job Market**.
